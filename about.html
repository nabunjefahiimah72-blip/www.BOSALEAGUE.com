Who We Are!

Our Story

The Bilal Alumni League started on the 5th of November 2023 by Mr. Habib Noha Doka, a former student of Bilal Islamic Institute from 2011–2016 with the aim of uniting all the former students in a football league that happens every Sunday at their sports center in Kabalagala from 9am to 4pm for a period of 4 months as a beacon of passion, resilience and unyielding dedication to the sport we all hold dear.

The league is in preparation for its third edition and we have so far registered twelve (12) teams and those are twelve (12) clusters (years). We call upon all old students from Bilal Islamic Institute to participate in the league whether as players, sponsors or supporters to stay united and continue selling the name and image of our noble institute.

Our Mission

Re-uniting old students of Bilal Islamic Institute to reconnect and come together to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive community committed to strengthening connections, for spirited competition and great camaraderie sparking new rivalries, and celebrating the shared spirit that never fades.

Our Vision

To unite the alumni of Bilal Islamic Institute through the spirit of football fostering lifelong brotherhood, sportsmanship ,and continuous bond with our alma mater
